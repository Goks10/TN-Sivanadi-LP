The only thing required from you is a clear photo of your thumb print — right thumb for men, left thumb for women. You send this via WhatsApp. We then search our Nadi bundle based on the thumb impression type. If your leaf is found, the reading is conducted via WhatsApp Video or Skype at a time that suits you.

We conduct readings in Tamil, Telugu, Hindi, and English. Please let us know your preferred language when booking.

A standard Nadi reading session typically lasts between 45 and 90 minutes, depending on how many Kandams are covered.

Our general matching and Kandam 1 reading starts from Rs. 3,000/-. This covers the Vaitheeswaran Koil library search, father/mother name verification, birth chart matching, and detailed future roadmap reading. If you require additional specific Kandams later, they can be added.

No. Remedies are mentioned only if they appear in your specific leaf. We explain what is written and leave all decisions entirely to you. There is no pressure at any stage.

Yes. We have served clients in UAE, USA, UK, Singapore, Australia, Malaysia, and many other countries. All sessions are conducted online and the thumb print is shared via WhatsApp.
